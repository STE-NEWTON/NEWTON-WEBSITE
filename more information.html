Limay National High School (LNHS) serves as a vital institution in the local community of Limay, Bataan, Philippines. Established in 1970, LNHS has a rich history of providing quality education to its students. The school is committed to academic excellence, offering a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of subjects, including mathematics, science, English, Filipino, social studies, and more.


Beyond academics, LNHS emphasizes holistic development, providing opportunities for students to engage in extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and cultural programs. These activities help students develop important life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and creativity.


LNHS is led by a dedicated team of administrators, teachers, and staff members who are committed to nurturing the talents and potentials of each student. The school also fosters a supportive learning environment that encourages collaboration and respect among students and educators.


Moreover, LNHS values community involvement and actively engages parents, alumni, and stakeholders in its educational initiatives. Through partnerships with various organizations and agencies, the school enhances its programs and resources to better serve its students and the community as a whole.


Overall, Limay National High School stands as a beacon of education in its locality, empowering generations of students to achieve their dreams and contribute positively to society.
