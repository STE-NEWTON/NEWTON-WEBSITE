STE (Science Technology Engineering)

Students of STE programs are typically passionate about exploring the realms of science and technology, particularly in the field of electronics. These students often possess strong analytical skills and a keen interest in understanding how electronic devices work.

SPA (Special Program in Arts

Students of SPA are typically characterized by their passion for artistic expression and creativity. They are individuals who possess a deep appreciation for various art forms and are eager to explore and develop their talents further.

SPS (Special Program in Sports

SPS students are typically highly motivated individuals who demonstrate dedication, discipline, and perseverance both on and off the field. They excel in balancing rigorous training schedules with academic responsibilities, often achieving success in both domains.

Regular Sections

Regular Program refers to the standard academic track followed by students in elementary and secondary education. It encompasses the mainstream curriculum prescribed by DepEd and includes subjects such as English, Mathematics, Science, Filipino, and others.
