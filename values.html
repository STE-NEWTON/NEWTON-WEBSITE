VALUES

Limay National High School is guided by our core values of integrity, excellence, and community. We aim to create an ideal atmosphere for students in which students are motivated to achieve their best and are socially responsible to their communities and the nation, thus promoting integrity, excellence, and community. We appreciate respect, discipline, and diversity, to make us good citizens who are socially aware and sensitive.


Our Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose competencies and values enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. We are learner-centered public institution that continuously improves itself to pursue its mission.



Our Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where: Students learn in a child-friendly gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen. Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.
